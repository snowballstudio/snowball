Snowball Privacy Policy

See the Interesting Side of Life

Last updated: 16 July 2026

English 中文版

1. Introduction

Snowball is an intelligent personal life visualization application that helps users understand the interesting side of everyday life through their own data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Snowball may access, how that information is used, where it is stored, and what choices users have.

Snowball is designed primarily as a local-data application. User records are stored on the user's device by default and are not uploaded to a server operated by Snowball Studio.

2. Information Snowball May Access

Depending on the functions selected and the permissions granted by the user, Snowball may access or process the following information:

  • Information entered voluntarily by the user.
  • Daily records, including food, mood and lifestyle information.
  • Step count and other authorised health-related data.
  • Device screen-time and application-usage statistics.
  • The list and identifiers of applications installed on the device.
  • Photos or images selected voluntarily by the user.
  • Voice input when the user actively uses a voice function.
  • Places and relationship information entered voluntarily by the user.

Snowball does not require users to create an account for its current local functions.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to provide features requested by the user, including:

  • Daily statistics and personal life summaries.
  • Personal life-data visualization.
  • Nutrition and lifestyle analysis.
  • Step-count and activity summaries.
  • Screen-time summaries and application-usage analysis.
  • Information Train visualization.
  • Footprint, object and relationship records.
  • Optional intelligent assistance in future versions.

Snowball does not use personal information for behavioural advertising, data brokerage or the sale of personal information.

4. App Usage Information

4.1 Usage Access

Snowball may request permission to access application-usage statistics. This information is used to calculate daily screen time, generate aggregated usage summaries and create the Information Train visualization.

Detailed application-usage information is processed locally on the device. The ordinary interface primarily presents aggregated summaries, recent results and leading applications rather than publicly displaying all detailed records.

A more detailed application-information page may be available only through a hidden developer mode for testing and advanced personal analysis. This information remains local and is not publicly disclosed.

4.2 Installed Application List

Snowball may read the installed application list to identify application names, group similar applications and classify them for screen-time summaries and visualization.

The installed application list is not used for advertising, user profiling or sale to third parties.

5. Health and Activity Data

Where supported and authorised, Snowball may read health or activity information such as step count through system or platform services, including services provided by Android, Huawei or Apple.

Health and activity data is used only to display personal statistics and support user-requested lifestyle visualization. Snowball does not provide medical diagnosis, medical treatment or emergency services.

Access to health data is optional and requires the user's permission. Users may continue to enter relevant information manually where this option is available.

6. Data Storage and Security

In the current version, personal records created in Snowball are stored locally on the user's device.

Snowball Studio does not operate a server that stores the user's personal life database. Local data may be removed if the user deletes records, clears the application's storage or uninstalls the application.

Users are responsible for maintaining suitable device security and for backing up information they wish to retain.

7. Optional AI Features

Future versions of Snowball may offer optional AI-powered functions. These functions will require an Internet connection.

When a user actively chooses to use an AI function, the text, voice, image or limited contextual information necessary to answer the current request may be transmitted to a selected third-party AI service provider.

Snowball will not automatically upload the user's complete historical life database to an AI service. Only information required for the user-initiated request should be transmitted.

Third-party AI providers may process submitted information according to their own privacy policies and service terms. This Privacy Policy will be updated when specific AI services are formally introduced.

8. Third-Party Services

Snowball may rely on operating-system or platform services for functions such as application-usage statistics, health data, voice input, image selection or future AI processing.

These services are governed by the privacy policies and terms of their respective providers. Snowball Studio does not control the independent data practices of third-party providers.

9. Permissions

Snowball may request permissions including:

  • Usage Access.
  • Access to the installed application list.
  • Physical-activity or health-data access.
  • Microphone access for user-initiated voice functions.
  • Photo or media access when the user selects an image.
  • Internet access for functions that require an online service.

Permissions are requested only when required for a relevant function. Users may refuse or revoke permissions through device settings, although certain functions may then be unavailable.

10. Advertising and Sale of Data

Snowball does not display third-party advertising in its current version.

Snowball Studio does not sell, rent or trade users' personal information.

11. Children's Privacy

Snowball is suitable for a general audience but is not designed exclusively for children.

Snowball Studio does not knowingly operate a service intended to collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians should supervise a child's use of the application where required by local law.

12. User Rights and Data Deletion

Users may:

  • Choose which optional information to enter.
  • Decline or revoke device permissions.
  • Edit or delete records within the application.
  • Clear the application's local storage.
  • Uninstall the application to remove locally stored application data.
  • Contact Snowball Studio with a privacy question or request.

Because current personal records are stored locally and are not held on a Snowball Studio server, Snowball Studio may not be able to recover or remotely delete data stored only on the user's device.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Snowball introduces new functions, integrates a new service or changes its data practices.

The latest version will be published on this page with an updated date. Material changes may also be communicated within the application or through an applicable app-store listing.

雪球隐私政策

看见生活的有趣

最后更新日期：2026年7月16日

一、简介

雪球是一款智能个人生活数据可视化应用，通过用户自己的数据，帮助用户发现日常生活中容易被忽略的有趣规律。

本隐私政策说明雪球可能访问哪些信息、这些信息如何使用、保存在哪里，以及用户享有哪些选择和权利。

雪球主要采用本地数据模式。用户记录默认保存在用户自己的设备中，不上传至由 Snowball Studio 运营的服务器。

二、雪球可能访问的信息

根据用户选择使用的功能以及授予的权限，雪球可能访问或处理以下信息：

  • 用户主动输入的信息。
  • 饮食、心情及生活方式等日常记录。
  • 步数及用户授权的其他健康或活动数据。
  • 设备屏幕时间和应用使用统计。
  • 设备中已安装应用的名称或标识信息。
  • 用户主动选择的照片或图片。
  • 用户主动使用语音功能时产生的语音输入。
  • 用户主动录入的地点、物品及人际关系信息。

雪球当前的本地功能不要求用户注册账户。

三、信息的用途

上述信息仅用于提供用户主动使用的功能，包括：

  • 每日统计和个人生活摘要。
  • 个人生活数据可视化。
  • 营养和生活方式分析。
  • 步数与活动情况汇总。
  • 屏幕时间汇总和应用使用分析。
  • “信息列车”可视化。
  • 足迹、物品和人际关系记录。
  • 未来版本中的可选智能辅助功能。

雪球不会将个人信息用于行为广告、数据交易或出售用户个人信息。

四、应用使用信息

4.1 应用使用情况访问权限

雪球可能请求访问应用使用统计的权限。相关信息用于计算每日屏幕时间、生成聚合后的使用摘要，以及绘制“信息列车”可视化内容。

详细的应用使用信息仅在设备本地处理。普通界面主要显示聚合结果、近期数据和排名靠前的应用，而不会默认向外公开全部详细记录。

更详细的应用信息页面可能仅通过隐藏的开发者模式开放，用于测试及高级个人分析。这些信息仍保留在本地，不会公开披露。

4.2 已安装应用列表

雪球可能读取设备中的已安装应用列表，用于识别应用名称、合并同类应用，并对应用进行分类，以生成屏幕时间摘要和可视化结果。

已安装应用列表不会用于广告、用户画像或向第三方出售。

五、健康与活动数据

在设备支持且用户授权的情况下，雪球可能通过 Android、华为或苹果等系统或平台服务读取步数等健康或活动数据。

健康与活动数据仅用于显示个人统计及用户主动使用的生活数据可视化。雪球不提供医疗诊断、医疗治疗或紧急救助服务。

健康数据权限属于可选权限，必须经用户授权。相关功能允许时，用户也可以选择手动录入数据。

六、数据存储与安全

在当前版本中，用户在雪球中创建的个人记录保存在用户自己的设备中。

Snowball Studio 不运营用于保存用户个人生活数据库的服务器。用户删除记录、清除应用存储或卸载应用时，本地数据可能被删除。

用户应妥善保护自己的设备，并自行备份希望长期保留的信息。

七、可选 AI 功能

雪球未来版本可能提供可选的 AI 智能功能。此类功能需要联网使用。

只有当用户主动使用 AI 功能时，为完成当前请求所必需的文本、语音、图片或有限的上下文信息，才可能传输给用户所选择的第三方 AI 服务提供商。

雪球不会自动把用户完整的历史生活数据库上传至 AI 服务。原则上仅发送处理当前用户请求所需要的信息。

第三方 AI 服务提供商可能按照其自身的隐私政策和服务条款处理用户提交的信息。雪球正式接入具体 AI 服务时，本隐私政策将同步更新。

八、第三方服务

雪球可能依赖操作系统或平台服务提供应用使用统计、健康数据、语音输入、图片选择或未来的 AI 处理功能。

这些服务受相应服务提供商的隐私政策和服务条款约束。Snowball Studio 无法控制第三方服务提供商独立的数据处理行为。

九、权限说明

雪球可能请求以下权限：

  • 应用使用情况访问权限。
  • 读取已安装应用列表的权限。
  • 身体活动或健康数据权限。
  • 用户主动使用语音功能时所需的麦克风权限。
  • 用户主动选择图片时所需的照片或媒体权限。
  • 使用在线服务时所需的网络权限。

雪球仅在相关功能确有需要时请求权限。用户可以拒绝或在设备设置中撤销权限，但部分对应功能可能因此无法使用。

十、广告与数据出售

雪球当前版本不展示第三方广告。

Snowball Studio 不会出售、出租或交易用户的个人信息。

十一、儿童隐私

雪球适合一般用户使用，但并不是专门面向儿童设计的应用。

Snowball Studio 不会明知而运营以收集儿童个人信息为目的的服务。当地法律有要求时，父母或监护人应监督儿童使用本应用。

十二、用户权利与数据删除

用户可以：

  • 自主决定是否录入可选信息。
  • 拒绝或撤销设备权限。
  • 在应用内编辑或删除记录。
  • 清除应用的本地存储数据。
  • 卸载应用，以删除保存在本机上的应用数据。
  • 就隐私问题或相关请求联系 Snowball Studio。

由于当前个人记录仅保存在用户设备中，并不由 Snowball Studio 的服务器持有，因此 Snowball Studio 可能无法恢复或远程删除仅存在于用户设备上的数据。

十三、隐私政策的更新

当雪球增加新功能、接入新服务或改变数据处理方式时，本隐私政策可能会更新。

最新版本将发布在本页面，并显示新的更新日期。重大变更也可能通过应用内提示或相关应用商店页面进行说明。
