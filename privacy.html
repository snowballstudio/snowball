Snowball Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Snowball is a personal life visualization application designed to help users record and understand their daily lives.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, Snowball may access:

  • Health data (such as step count)
  • App usage statistics
  • Installed application list
  • Information entered by the user

3. How Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide app features such as:

  • Daily statistics
  • Life visualization
  • Nutrition analysis
  • Usage summaries

4. Data Storage

Currently, Snowball stores user data locally on the user's device. No personal information is uploaded to the developer's server.

Future versions may provide optional AI-powered features that require Internet access. If cloud services are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Permissions

Snowball may request permissions including:

  • Usage Access
  • Installed Apps
  • Health Data

These permissions are used only to generate personal statistics and visualizations within the app.

6. Third-party Services

Future versions may integrate AI services or Health platforms. Their privacy practices will be governed by their own policies.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time by deleting records within the app or uninstalling the application.
